Privacy Policy

nihongo.fm's privacy policy covers the collection and use of personal information that may be collected by nihongo.fm anytime you interact with nihongo.fm, such as when you visit the nihongo.fm website, join the email newsletter, or purchase nihongo.fm products or services. Your privacy is taken seriously at nihongo.fm, and we work hard to protect it.

nihongo.fm takes precautions (administrative, technical, and physical) to safeguard your personal information.

nihongo.fm does not sell or rent your personal contact information to other marketers.

The nihongo.fm shopping cart uses Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption on all web pages where personal information is input. To make purchases from the MP3 Store, you have to use an SSL-enabled browser such as Safari, Firefox, Netscape Navigator 3.0 or later, or Internet Explorer. This protects the confidentiality of your personal and credit card information while transmitted over the Internet. No credit card information is kept on our servers.

Like most web sites, nihongo.fm gathers certain information automatically and store it in log files. We use this information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze trends & to track users’movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our users. nihongo.fm will not use the information collected to market directly to that person. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and click path data.

nihongo.fm is not responsible for privacy practices of linked sites. Please learn about the privacy policies of those companies.

If required by law or litigation to disclose your personal information we will. We may also disclose information about you if we determine that for national security, law enforcement, or other issues of public importance, disclosure is necessary.

nihongo.fm may update its privacy policy occasionally. When we change the policy this page will be updated.
