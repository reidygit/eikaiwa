About nihongo.fm

nihongo.fm streams Japanese study material, nihongo industry news and information 24 hours a day from Yokohama, Japan. nihongo.fm's English to Japanese streaming audio content lets you study Japanese when you want for as long as you want. nihongo.fm downloads lets you take your Japanese study segments with you on your iPod or mp3 player.

Use nihongo.fm to help pass your Japanese test, communicate better in business, improve your Japanese pronunciation or learn to make small talk with your Japanese friends. At work, school, home or anywhere you connect to the Internet - nihongo.fm is there for you.
How to Use

Studying with nihongo.fm is easy. No textbooks or other materials are needed. nihongo.fm is your hands-free Japanese learning station. Just tune in, listen, repeat and repeat again. Each study segment presents one topic or category at a time. Study segments are kept to a managable time length (usually between 3 to 5 minutes) so listeners are not overwhelmed (or don't drift off).

If you miss something in the FREE or PREMIUM nihongo.fm broadcast and need to hear it again, you can download any study segment from nihongo.fm’s downloads store. There you'll find full length, high quality ad-free mp3’s for your iPod, computer or mp3 player.

In each study segment, the target words or sentences are presented once in English followed by the Japanese version two times. The silence following the first rendering of the target word or sentence is for repeating orally or mentally one time. The longer silence following the second rendering is for repeating more times if necessary.

Words or sentences with multiple translations of one word will use the Japanese term "arui wa" which means "or" in Japanese.

For best results when using a computer, connect quality speakers to your computer or connect your computer to your home audio system. Please consult your computer owners manual for details. iPods and other mp3 players should try different settings on the player's equilizer (EQ).
